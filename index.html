Welcome to LimSwap!

Trade your Roblox Limited items with ease and flexibility!

Why Choose Us?

Unlike the official Roblox trade system, we don't have a trade hold or a daily trade limit. This means you can trade as often as you like, and your items are available for trade immediately after you receive them.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a safe, easy, and efficient platform for trading Roblox Limited items. We believe in the power of the Roblox trading community and want to help it thrive by removing barriers to trade.

FAQ

How does this work?

You link your Roblox account, deposit items, and then trade those items with other users throughout the site. When you're ready, you can withdraw your items to your Roblox account!

How can I withdraw my items?

You can withdraw your items by clicking on the 'Withdraw' button in the navigation bar. Follow the instructions to complete the withdrawal process.

What is the minimum value of items I can trade?

The minimum value of items you can trade is 200 RAP.
